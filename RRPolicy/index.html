Resume Radar FAQs:

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs):

  1. What is Resume Radar?

    Resume Radar is an AI-powered Chrome extension that analyzes your resume against job descriptions to help you get more interviews. It provides match scores, tailored suggestions for improvement, and AI-powered resume optimization.

  2. How do the subscriptions work?

    Resume Radar offers: ‚Ä¢ Free tier: Limited to 3 resume analyses per month ‚Ä¢ Pro subscription ($14.99): Full access to all features for 14 days

  3. How do I use Resume Radar?

    Simply install the extension, navigate to a job posting, open the Resume Radar popup, and either paste your resume text or upload a PDF/DOCX file. The extension will automatically detect job descriptions on supported job sites and analyze your resume against them.

  4. Which job sites are supported?

    Resume Radar currently supports LinkedIn, Indeed, ZipRecruiter, Dice, BuiltIn, Glassdoor, Monster, CareerBuilder, SimplyHired, Greenhouse, Lever, Workday, SmartRecruiters, Jobvite, Wellfound, Y Combinator, Stack Overflow Jobs, and more.

  5. How accurate is the AI analysis?

    Our AI uses advanced language models to analyze the match between your resume and job descriptions. While it's highly effective at identifying relevant keywords and suggesting improvements, we recommend using it as a complementary tool to your own judgment and industry knowledge.

  6. Can I generate an optimized resume?

    Yes, the Pro version allows you to generate a completely optimized version of your resume tailored to specific job descriptions. You can then download this as plain text, PDF, or DOCX.

  7. How is my resume data stored and used?

    We take your privacy seriously. Your resume and analysis results are stored securely in your browser's local storage and our secure cloud database. We do not sell or share your resume data with third parties. Resume data is only used to provide the analysis service and improve our algorithms.

  8. Why might analysis results vary?

    As an AI-powered service, analysis results may vary slightly for the same resume and job description. This is normal and reflects the AI's consideration of different factors during each analysis. For consistent results, you can save analyses for future reference.

  9. What happens if the analysis fails?

    In rare cases where analysis fails (due to network issues or service interruptions), the attempt won't count against your monthly limit. You can simply try again. If problems persist, try refreshing the page or contact support.

  10. Can I use Resume Radar on multiple devices?

    Yes, your Pro subscription is linked to your Google account. Simply log in with the same Google account on any device where you've installed Resume Radar, and your subscription will be automatically applied.

Hev LLC ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our applications: Pathsight, GymLens, and Eggsplore (collectively, the "Services").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide to Us

We may collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we may collect your name, email address, phone number (Eggsplore), and password.
  • Profile Information: Your profile picture, preferences, and settings.
  • Transaction Information: If you purchase a subscription, we collect payment information, billing address, and transaction history.
  • User Content:
    • Pathsight: Route history, saved locations
    • GymLens: Equipment photos, workout history
    • Eggsplore: Listings, ratings, messages
  • Communication: Messages, emails, or other communications between you and Hev LLC.

1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

When you use our Services, we automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Usage Data: How you interact with our Services, including which features you use.
  • Device Information: Information about your device, including device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, mobile network information, and IP address.
  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect location data to:
    • Pathsight: Provide navigation services, route planning, and weather information
    • GymLens: For regional content customization (optional)
    • Eggsplore: Show nearby listings and enable location-based services
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our Services and hold certain information.

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Services.

  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns and trends to enhance the user experience.
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activities, and protect the rights and property of Hev LLC and others.

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process. Pathsight Privacy Details

This section provides specific privacy details for Pathsight, our AI-powered navigation app.

Data Collection Specific to Pathsight

  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect precise location data to provide navigation services, route planning, and nearby points of interest information.
  • Route History: We store your route history to enable you to access your saved routes.
  • Search Queries: Your destination searches and route preferences.
  • AI Interaction: We process your inputs with Google Gemini to generate route suggestions and navigation instructions.
  • Weather Data: We access weather information via third-party services based on your location to provide weather-informed routing.

How We Use Pathsight Data

  • Generate personalized navigation routes
  • Provide real-time travel insights and directions
  • Offer weather-informed routing suggestions
  • Save your route history for convenient access
  • Improve route algorithms and AI suggestions

Third-Party Services for Pathsight

  • Google Gemini API: Used for AI-powered route generation and direction creation
  • OpenWeather API: Provides weather information along routes
  • Apple Maps: Used for map visualization and navigation
  • Apple StoreKit: Manages subscription purchases

Safety Note: Pathsight cannot guarantee 100% safety or accuracy of route suggestions. Always prioritize official traffic regulations and exercise caution when traveling.

GymLens Privacy Details

This section provides specific privacy details for GymLens, our AI-powered fitness app.

Data Collection Specific to GymLens

  • Equipment Photos: Images you take or upload of workout equipment for analysis. These are processed to generate workout recommendations.
  • Fitness Preferences: Your fitness goals, limitations, and equipment preferences.
  • Workout History: For subscribers, we store your workout history to track progress.
  • Device Information: Information about your device to optimize app performance.

How We Use GymLens Data

  • Analyze equipment photos to identify available gear
  • Generate personalized workout routines
  • Track workout history for progress monitoring
  • Improve exercise recognition algorithms
  • Enhance workout recommendations based on user feedback

Image Processing and Storage

Images are processed securely for the purpose of equipment recognition. By default, uploaded images are not stored long-term unless you explicitly save a workout. Images are never shared with third parties or used for purposes other than workout generation and app improvement.

Third-Party Services for GymLens

  • Apple StoreKit: Manages subscription purchases
  • CloudKit: Optional synchronization of workouts across your devices

Health Disclaimer: GymLens is not a medical device and does not store or process protected health information ("PHI") as defined by HIPAA. Always consult with healthcare professionals before starting any new exercise program.

Eggsplore Privacy Details

This section provides specific privacy details for Eggsplore, our local egg marketplace app.

Data Collection Specific to Eggsplore

  • Phone Number: Used for account verification via SMS code.
  • Profile Information: Your name, location, and any profile details you choose to share.
  • Listing Data: Information about eggs you list for sale (descriptions, photos, prices, etc.).
  • Location Data: Used to show nearby listings and facilitate local transactions.
  • Messages: Communication between buyers and sellers.
  • Ratings and Reviews: Feedback about sellers and buyers.

How We Use Eggsplore Data

  • Verify your identity through phone number verification
  • Display your listings to potential buyers
  • Show you listings from sellers in your area
  • Facilitate communication between buyers and sellers
  • Process premium subscription payments

Phone Number Privacy

Your phone number is used for account verification and is not publicly displayed to other users unless you choose to include it in your listings or share it directly with other users. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your phone number.

Third-Party Services for Eggsplore

  • Firebase: Used for authentication, storage, and database services
  • Apple StoreKit: Manages subscription purchases

Transaction Safety: While we provide the platform for users to connect, we are not responsible for the quality of eggs or transactions between users. We recommend following food safety guidelines and exercising caution when meeting other users for transactions.

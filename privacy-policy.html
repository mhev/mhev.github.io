Boozly Privacy Details

This section provides specific privacy details for Boozly, our AI-powered cocktail recipe suggestion app.

Data Collection Specific to Boozly

  • Bar/Shelf Photos: Images you take or upload of your home bar, liquor shelf, or bottles for analysis. These are processed to identify ingredients and generate cocktail suggestions.
  • Saved Recipes: Cocktail recipes you choose to save are stored locally on your device.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous usage metrics to track free scan limits and improve the app experience.
  • Subscription Information: For subscribers, we process subscription status through Apple's StoreKit.
  • Device Information: Basic device information for app optimization and crash reporting.

How We Use Boozly Data

  • Analyze photos of your bar to identify bottles, spirits, mixers, and ingredients
  • Generate personalized cocktail recipe suggestions based on identified ingredients
  • Store your saved recipes locally for convenient access
  • Track usage limits for free users to prevent API abuse
  • Process and manage subscription purchases
  • Improve our ingredient recognition and recipe recommendation algorithms

Image Processing and Storage

Photos you take or upload are sent securely to OpenAI's API for analysis to identify bottles and ingredients. Images are processed in real-time and are not stored permanently on our servers. OpenAI processes images according to their privacy policy and data usage terms. We do not use your images for training purposes or share them with any other third parties.

Local Data Storage

Saved recipes and usage tracking data are stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms (Keychain for usage data, UserDefaults for recipes). This data persists across app updates but can be cleared by uninstalling the app.

Third-Party Services for Boozly

  • OpenAI API: Used for AI-powered image analysis to identify bottles and ingredients, and to generate cocktail recipe suggestions
  • Apple StoreKit: Manages subscription purchases, free trials, and payment processing

Age Requirement and Responsible Use

Boozly is intended for users of legal drinking age in their respective jurisdictions. By using Boozly, you confirm that you are of legal drinking age in your country or region. The app includes an age verification gate that requires users to confirm they are of legal drinking age before accessing the app's features.

Important Alcohol-Related Disclaimers:

  • Age Restriction: Boozly is strictly intended for adults of legal drinking age (21+ in the United States, or the legal drinking age in your jurisdiction). We do not promote, encourage, or condone underage drinking.
  • Drink Responsibly: We encourage all users to consume alcohol responsibly and in moderation. Never drink and drive. Know your limits.
  • No Alcohol Sales: Boozly does not sell, distribute, or facilitate the purchase of alcohol. We only provide cocktail recipe suggestions based on ingredients you already own.
  • Health Considerations: If you are pregnant, nursing, taking medications, or have health conditions affected by alcohol consumption, please consult a healthcare professional before consuming alcoholic beverages.
  • Recipe Accuracy: While we strive to provide accurate cocktail recipes, we cannot guarantee the accuracy of all recipes. Users should use their own judgment when preparing and consuming cocktails.
  • No Liability: Hev LLC is not responsible for any consequences resulting from the use of recipes provided by Boozly, including but not limited to overconsumption, allergic reactions, or any harm caused by alcohol consumption.

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Photos: Processed in real-time and not stored permanently
  • Saved Recipes: Stored locally until you delete them or uninstall the app
  • Usage Data: Stored in device Keychain to persist across reinstalls for abuse prevention
  • Subscription Data: Managed by Apple and retained according to their policies

Your Responsibility: By using Boozly, you acknowledge that you are solely responsible for your alcohol consumption decisions. Boozly provides recipe suggestions only and does not encourage excessive drinking or any illegal activity related to alcohol.

Poli-gone Privacy Details

This section provides specific privacy details for Poli-gone, our browser extension that replaces political content with positive alternatives.

Data Collection Specific to Poli-gone

  • Content Analysis Data: Text content from web pages is analyzed locally to detect political content. This analysis happens entirely within your browser.
  • Extension Settings: Your preferences for content filtering sensitivity, replacement content types, and whitelisted websites.
  • Usage Statistics: Anonymous statistics about content filtering activity (number of items filtered, websites visited) to improve the extension.
  • Browser Information: Basic browser and extension version information for compatibility and updates.
  • Payment Information: For premium users, subscription status and payment processing data through Polar.sh.

How We Use Poli-gone Data

  • Analyze web page content to identify political posts, articles, and discussions
  • Replace detected political content with kitten facts, images, or other positive content
  • Store your filtering preferences and settings locally in your browser
  • Process premium subscription payments and manage access to advanced features
  • Improve content detection algorithms through anonymous usage patterns
  • Provide customer support and technical assistance

Privacy-First Design

Poli-gone is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or share your personal browsing history, the specific content you view, or any personal information. All content analysis happens locally in your browser using a hybrid approach that combines keyword detection (free) with AI analysis (premium) only when needed.

Content Processing

When you visit websites, Poli-gone scans the page content locally to identify political content. For premium users, uncertain content may be sent to Google Gemini API for AI analysis, but this is done with strict cost controls and usage limits. The content is not stored or used for training purposes.

Third-Party Services for Poli-gone

  • Google Gemini API: Used for AI-powered content analysis (premium feature only)
  • Polar.sh: Handles subscription payments and user management
  • Chrome/Safari Extension APIs: Required for browser extension functionality
  • Local Storage: Your settings and preferences are stored locally in your browser

Data Storage and Retention

Your extension settings and preferences are stored locally in your browser and are not transmitted to our servers. Usage statistics are anonymized and used only for improving the extension's performance. Premium subscription data is handled securely by Polar.sh according to their privacy policy.

Content Replacement Sources

Poli-gone replaces political content with positive alternatives including kitten facts, images, and trivia content. This replacement content is sourced from curated databases and is designed to create a more positive browsing experience.

Privacy Commitment: Poli-gone does not track your browsing habits, collect personal information, or share your data with third parties. All processing happens locally in your browser, and we maintain strict privacy controls to protect your online experience.

Yap Cal Privacy Details

This section provides specific privacy details for Yap Cal, our AI-powered voice calorie tracking app.

Data Collection Specific to Yap Cal

  • Voice Input: Audio recordings of your food descriptions are processed on-device using Apple's Speech Recognition framework. Voice data is converted to text locally and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • Food Descriptions: Text descriptions of meals you log (via voice or text input) are sent to AI services for calorie estimation.
  • Calorie & Nutrition Logs: Your logged meals, calorie counts, and macro breakdowns (protein, carbs, fat) are stored locally on your device using SwiftData.
  • Previously Logged Foods: Foods you've logged are saved locally for quick re-logging convenience.
  • Goal Settings: Your calorie and macro goals are stored locally in UserDefaults.
  • Device Information: Basic device information for app optimization and crash reporting.

How We Use Yap Cal Data

  • Convert voice input to text using on-device speech recognition
  • Send food descriptions to AI services for calorie and macro estimation
  • Look up brand-specific nutrition information for accurate estimates
  • Store your food logs locally to track daily and historical intake
  • Save previously logged foods for convenient quick-logging
  • Display your progress toward daily calorie and macro goals
  • Provide Home Screen widget data showing today's calorie count

Voice Processing and Privacy

Yap Cal uses Apple's on-device Speech Recognition framework to convert your voice to text. This processing happens entirely on your device ‚Äî your voice recordings are never sent to Hev LLC servers. Only the resulting text description is sent to AI services for calorie estimation.

AI-Powered Calorie Estimation

When you log a meal, the text description is sent to OpenAI's API to estimate calories and macronutrients. For branded foods (e.g., "Chick-fil-A sandwich"), we may also query Perplexity's API to retrieve accurate nutrition information. These API calls include only the food description ‚Äî no personal information, device identifiers, or usage history is transmitted.

Local Data Storage

All your food logs, nutrition history, goal settings, and previously logged foods are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework and UserDefaults. This data is not uploaded to any cloud service. Uninstalling the app will delete all stored data.

Third-Party Services for Yap Cal

  • Apple Speech Recognition: On-device voice-to-text conversion (no data transmitted externally)
  • OpenAI API: AI-powered calorie and macro estimation from food descriptions
  • Perplexity API: Brand-specific nutrition lookup for accurate estimates
  • Apple WidgetKit: Home Screen widget displaying daily calorie progress

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Voice Recordings: Processed on-device in real-time and not stored
  • Food Logs: Stored locally until you delete them or uninstall the app
  • Previously Logged Foods: Stored locally, limited to 50 most recent items
  • Goal Settings: Stored locally until you change them or uninstall the app

Privacy-First Design: Yap Cal is designed to keep your data on your device. We do not create user accounts, do not sync data to the cloud, and do not track your eating habits beyond what's stored locally on your device. Your nutrition journey is yours alone.

Health Disclaimer: Yap Cal provides AI-generated calorie and nutrition estimates for informational purposes only. These estimates may not be 100% accurate. Yap Cal is not a medical device and should not be used as a substitute for professional dietary advice. Always consult with healthcare professionals for personalized nutrition guidance.

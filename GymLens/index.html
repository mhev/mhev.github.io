  4. Q: What equipment can GymLens recognize?
    A: We detect dumbbells, barbells, kettlebells, bands, machines, benches, cardio gear, and more.
  5. Q: How accurate are the suggestions?
    A: Our AI follows fitness best practices, but suggestions are guidance only. Always consult a qualified professional for personalized advice.

  7. Q: Why might suggestions vary?
    A: Variation helps avoid plateaus by offering diverse exercises for the same equipment.

  9. Q: How is my privacy protected?
    A: Images are processed securely, not permanently stored unless you save a workout, and never shared externally.

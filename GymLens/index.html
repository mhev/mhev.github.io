GymLens FAQs

  1. Q: What is GymLens?
    A: GymLens is an AI-powered fitness app that analyzes photos of workout equipment to generate personalized routines based on available gear and your goals.
  2. Q: How does the subscription work?
    A: We offer:
    • 3-day free trial with full access
    • $4.99 per month after trial
    • $24.99 per year (~$2.08/mo) after trial
  3. Q: How do I use GymLens?
    A: Take/upload a clear photo of your equipment, select targets or limitations, and let GymLens craft your workout.
  4. Q: What gear is recognized?
    A: Dumbbells, barbells, kettlebells, bands, machines, benches, cardio gear, and more.
  5. Q: How accurate are the suggestions?
    A: AI follows fitness best practices but is guidance only. Always consult a professional for personalized advice.
  6. Q: Can I save workouts?
    A: Yes‚Äîsubscribers can save and track workouts over time.
  7. Q: Why do suggestions vary?
    A: Variation avoids plateaus by rotating exercises for the same equipment.
  8. Q: What if equipment isn't recognized?
    A: Ensure good lighting and a clear frame. You can also add notes manually before generating.
  9. Q: How is my privacy protected?
    A: Images are processed securely, not stored long-term unless you save a workout, and never shared externally.

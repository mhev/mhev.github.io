Boozly Support

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Boozly?

Boozly is an AI-powered cocktail recipe app. Simply snap a photo of your home bar, liquor shelf, or available ingredients, and Boozly will identify what you have and suggest cocktails you can make right now‚Äîno missing ingredients, no impossible recipes.

How does the subscription work?

We offer:

  • 3-day free trial with full access
  • $3.99 per month after trial
  • $19.99 per year (~$1.67/mo) after trial

How do I use Boozly?

Open the app, take or upload a photo of your bar or bottles, and wait a few seconds. Boozly's AI will analyze the image, identify your ingredients, and present a list of cocktails you can make. Tap any cocktail to see the full recipe with step-by-step instructions.

What ingredients does Boozly recognize?

Boozly can recognize a wide variety of spirits (vodka, gin, rum, whiskey, tequila, etc.), liqueurs, mixers, juices, sodas, garnishes, and common bar ingredients. For best results, ensure bottles are visible and labels are facing the camera.

Why didn't Boozly recognize my bottles?

Recognition works best with good lighting and clearly visible labels. Try repositioning bottles so labels face the camera, ensure adequate lighting, and avoid blurry photos. If issues persist, try taking a closer photo of specific bottles.

Can I save recipes for later?

Yes! Subscribers can save their favorite cocktail recipes. Tap "Save Recipe" at the bottom of any recipe detail view. Access your saved recipes anytime from the Settings menu under "Saved Recipes."

How accurate are the cocktail recipes?

Boozly's AI provides recipes based on classic cocktail formulations and modern mixology. While we strive for accuracy, recipes are for informational purposes. Feel free to adjust proportions to your taste preferences.

Why do I see different cocktail suggestions each time?

Our AI generates varied suggestions to help you discover new drinks. Even with the same ingredients, you might see different cocktail recommendations to keep things interesting and expand your mixology repertoire.

Do I get a free scan before subscribing?

Yes! You get one free scan to try Boozly before subscribing. After that, you'll need to start your 3-day free trial or subscribe to continue using the app.

How do I restore my subscription?

If you've previously subscribed, tap "Restore Purchases" on the subscription screen. Your subscription is linked to your Apple ID and will be restored automatically. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

How do I redeem a promo code?

On the subscription screen, tap "Redeem Code" at the bottom. This will open Apple's promo code redemption sheet where you can enter your code. Once redeemed, your subscription will be activated.

Is there an age requirement to use Boozly?

Yes. Boozly is intended for users of legal drinking age only (21+ in the United States, or the legal drinking age in your jurisdiction). You must confirm your age when first opening the app.

Does Boozly sell alcohol?

No. Boozly is a recipe suggestion app only. We do not sell, distribute, or facilitate the purchase of alcohol. Recipes are based on ingredients you already own.

How is my photo data handled?

Photos are processed securely through our AI service for ingredient recognition. Images are analyzed in real-time and are not stored permanently on our servers. We never share your photos with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel, go to your iPhone Settings ‚Üí tap your name ‚Üí Subscriptions ‚Üí Boozly ‚Üí Cancel Subscription. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period. Cancel at least 24 hours before renewal to avoid being charged.

Martina Support

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Martina?

Martina is a professional pet care scheduling and route management app designed for dog walkers, pet sitters, and pet care services. It helps you organize visits, optimize routes, and manage your clients efficiently.

How do I add a new visit?

Tap the floating action button (+ icon) in the bottom-right corner of your schedule. Fill in the pet's information, select the service type (walk, sitting, drop-in, overnight), set the time, and enter the address. The app will automatically geocode the location.

How does route optimization work?

Martina uses AI-powered route optimization to suggest the most efficient travel order for your daily visits. The system considers your home base location, visit times, and traffic patterns to minimize travel time and fuel costs.

How do I complete a visit?

Swipe left on any visit card or tap the visit to open the detail view, then tap "Complete." You'll see a confirmation dialog to prevent accidental completions. Completed visits are visually marked and move to your completion history.

Can I manage multiple clients and pets?

Yes! Martina supports unlimited clients and pets. Each visit is linked to a specific pet and owner, with full contact information and service history. You can view all your saved clients in the Profile section.

How do I use the map features?

The map view shows all your visits as clustered markers, with your home base clearly marked. Tap any marker to see visit details and get directions. The app integrates with Apple Maps for turn-by-turn navigation and real-time traffic updates.

What are the different service types?

Martina supports four main service types: Walk (outdoor exercise), Sitting (in-home care), Drop-in (quick check-ins), and Overnight (extended stays). Each type has its own color coding and icon for easy identification.

How is my data stored and protected?

All your data is stored locally on your device and backed up to your iCloud account. Client information, visit history, and routes are never shared with third parties. You maintain full control over your business data.

Can I export my visit data?

Yes! Martina includes export functionality in the Profile section. You can export your schedule data for record-keeping, invoicing, or transferring to other business systems.

What if I need help with the app?

Contact our support team at support@hevllc.com for assistance. We're here to help you get the most out of Martina and grow your pet care business.

App Information

Martina is currently in development and will be available soon on the App Store. Key features include:

  • Smart Scheduling: Time-grouped visits with easy management
  • Route Optimization: AI-powered travel planning
  • Client Management: Comprehensive pet and owner profiles
  • Map Integration: Real-time navigation and traffic updates
  • Data Export: Professional reporting and record-keeping

How do I create an account?

To create an account, simply open Eggsplore and enter your phone number. You'll receive a verification code via SMS. Once verified, you can complete your profile by adding your name and location.

How do I post a listing?

To post a listing, tap the "+" tab at the bottom of the screen. Fill in your listing details including title, price, quantity, egg type, and description. You can also add photos and set your location. Your phone number must be verified before posting.

How do I contact a seller?

When viewing a listing, tap the "Contact Seller" button. You'll be able to message the seller directly through the app to discuss the purchase.

How do I rate a seller?

After interacting with a seller, you can rate them by visiting their listing and tapping the "Rate Seller" button. You can provide a rating out of 5 stars and leave a comment about your experience.

How do I edit or delete my listing?

To edit or delete your listing, navigate to the listing detail page. If you're the owner, you'll see "Edit" and "Delete" buttons at the bottom of the screen.

How do I filter listings?

Use the filters at the top of the home screen to sort by distance, price, or newest. You can also filter by egg type and other characteristics like organic feed and free-range.

What are Premium features?

Premium users get the ability to feature their listings, receive push notifications for new listings to get early access, and display a premium badge on their Profile.

Poli-gone Support

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Poli-gone?

Poli-gone is a browser extension that automatically detects and replaces political content with adorable kitten facts and images, creating a more positive and stress-free browsing experience across social media and news websites.

How does Poli-gone work?

Poli-gone uses AI-powered content detection to identify political posts, articles, and discussions. When detected, it seamlessly replaces them with cute kitten content, fun facts, and uplifting images to create a more positive online experience.

Which websites does Poli-gone work on?

Poli-gone works on all major websites including social media platforms, news sites, blogs, and forums. It's designed to work across the entire web to provide consistent content filtering wherever you browse.

Can I customize what content gets replaced?

Yes! Poli-gone includes customizable settings that allow you to adjust the sensitivity of political content detection, choose between different replacement content types (kittens, trivia, etc.), and even whitelist specific websites where you want to see all content.

How accurate is the political content detection?

Our AI detection system is highly accurate and continuously improved. However, like any AI system, it may occasionally miss some political content or flag non-political content. You can always report false positives or negatives through the extension's feedback system.

Does Poli-gone collect my personal data?

No. Poli-gone is designed with privacy in mind. We don't collect, store, or share your personal data, browsing history, or the content you view. All processing happens locally in your browser.

Can I temporarily disable Poli-gone?

Yes! You can easily toggle Poli-gone on and off using the extension icon in your browser toolbar. This allows you to temporarily see all content when needed, such as when you want to read important political news.

What types of replacement content does Poli-gone provide?

Poli-gone offers a variety of positive content including cute kitten photos, fun cat facts, interesting trivia, motivational quotes, and uplifting images. You can customize your preferences to see more of what you enjoy.

Does Poli-gone work on mobile browsers?

Currently, Poli-gone is available for Chrome and other Chromium-based browsers on desktop. Mobile browser support is planned for future updates.

How do I install Poli-gone?

You can install Poli-gone from the Chrome Web Store. Simply search for "Poli-gone" or visit our store page, click "Add to Chrome," and follow the installation prompts. The extension will be ready to use immediately.

What if I encounter issues with the extension?

If you experience any problems, try refreshing the webpage or restarting your browser. For persistent issues, you can contact our support team at support@hevllc.com or use the feedback feature in the extension's options page.

Installation & Usage

Getting started with Poli-gone is simple:

  1. Install: Download from the Chrome Web Store
  2. Activate: The extension works automatically after installation
  3. Customize: Access settings via the extension icon to personalize your experience
  4. Enjoy: Browse the web with a more positive, stress-free experience

Poli-gone is completely free to use with no subscriptions or hidden costs.

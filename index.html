Pathsight FAQs:

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs):

  1. What is Pathsight?

    Pathsight is an AI-powered route planning and navigation assistance application that provides detailed, conversational directions and travel insights.

  2. How do the subscriptions work?

    Pathsight offers different subscription tiers: • Free tier includes 5 routes total • Basic subscription ($3.99/month) includes 200 routes per month • Pro subscription ($7.99/month) includes 500 routes per month

  3. How do I use the AI route planning?

    Simply enter your start and end points, and Pathsight will use AI to generate detailed route suggestions with natural language directions and travel insights.

  4. Can I save my routes?

    Yes, premium subscribers can save their favorite routes and access them later in their route history.

  5. Why might route suggestions vary?

    As an AI-powered service, route suggestions may vary slightly even for the same start and end points. This is normal and reflects the AI's ability to consider multiple factors and generate diverse recommendations. You can enable "Main route only" mode for more consistent results.

  6. How accurate is the AI-generated content?

    While our AI (powered by DeepSeek) strives for accuracy, all suggestions should be used as guidance only. Always verify critical information, especially regarding road conditions, business hours, or travel restrictions. The AI provides helpful insights but should not be your sole source of navigation information.

  7. What is "Main route only" mode?

    This is a faster mode that generates only the primary recommended route instead of multiple alternatives. It's useful when you want quick, straightforward directions without additional options.

  8. How current is the route information?

    The AI generates routes based on its training data and integrates with Apple Maps for navigation. While major roads and routes are generally accurate, always verify current conditions, especially for temporary closures or recent changes. Weather information is provided in real-time through OpenWeather API.

  9. What happens if the AI generation fails?

    In rare cases where route generation fails (due to network issues or service interruptions), the attempt won't count against your monthly route limit. You can simply try again. If problems persist, try enabling "Main route only" mode or contact support.

  10. How is my privacy protected when using the AI features?

    While we use DeepSeek's API for route generation, we only share the minimum necessary information (such as start/end points and preferences). Your routes are stored securely in our system, but interactions with DeepSeek's API are subject to their privacy policy and data handling practices. We encourage you to review DeepSeek's privacy policy for more information about how they handle data.

Eggsplore FAQs:

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs):

  1. How do I create an account?

    To create an account, simply open Eggsplore and enter your phone number. You'll receive a verification code via SMS. Once verified, you can complete your profile by adding your name and location.

  2. How do I post a listing?

    To post a listing, tap the "+" tab at the bottom of the screen. Fill in your listing details including title, price, quantity, egg type, and description. You can also add photos and set your location. Your phone number must be verified before posting.

  3. How do I contact a seller?

    When viewing a listing, tap the "Contact Seller" button. You'll be able to message the seller directly through the app to discuss the purchase.

  4. How do I rate a seller?

    After interacting with a seller, you can rate them by visiting their listing and tapping the "Rate Seller" button. You can provide a rating out of 5 stars and leave a comment about your experience.

  5. How do I edit or delete my listing?

    To edit or delete your listing, navigate to the listing detail page. If you're the owner, you'll see "Edit" and "Delete" buttons at the bottom of the screen.

  6. How do I filter listings?

    Use the filters at the top of the home screen to sort by distance, price, or newest. You can also filter by egg type and other characteristics like organic feed and free-range.

  7. What are Premium features?

    Premium users get the ability to feature their listings, receive push notifications for new listings to get early access, and display a premium badge on their Profile.

  8. How do I get Premium?

    Premium features can be purchased through the App Store.

Wrist Jester FAQs:

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs):

  1. How do I set the number of jokes I receive per day?

    To set the number of jokes you receive per day, open the Wrist Jester app, tap on the settings icon, and adjust the frequency slider from 0 to 10 jokes per day.

  2. How do I get an instant joke?

    To get an instant joke, simply open the Wrist Jester app and tap the "Tap to get a joke!" button on the main screen. You'll receive a joke right away.

  3. What is the source of the jokes?

    The jokes in Wrist Jester are provided by the Official Joke API. We use this API to bring you a variety of random jokes throughout the day.

  4. How do I stop receiving jokes?

    To stop receiving jokes, go to the settings page and set the frequency to 0. This will disable the notifications for daily jokes.

  5. How do I access the Terms and Conditions?

    You can access the Terms and Conditions by tapping the "Terms and Conditions" button in the Info section of the Wrist Jester app.

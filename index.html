TickleTap FAQs:

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs):

  1. How do I set the number of jokes I receive per day?

    To set the number of jokes you receive per day, open the TickleTap app, tap on the settings icon, and adjust the frequency slider from 0 to 10 jokes per day.

  2. How do I get an instant joke?

    To get an instant joke, simply open the TickleTap app and tap the "Tap to get a joke!" button on the main screen. You'll receive a joke right away.

  3. What is the source of the jokes?

    The jokes in TickleTap are provided by the Official Joke API. We use this API to bring you a variety of random jokes throughout the day.

  4. How do I stop receiving jokes?

    To stop receiving jokes, go to the settings page and set the frequency to 0. This will disable the notifications for daily jokes.

  5. How do I access the Terms and Conditions?

    You can access the Terms and Conditions by tapping the "Terms and Conditions" button in the Info section of the TickleTap app.
